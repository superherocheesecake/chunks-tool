Chunk generator rules:

Floor: 1 floor piece is always 4 grid points wide. Want 2 floor pieces at 8 floor grid points. 6 grid points will generate 1 floor and 2 empty tiles.

Hole: Min. 5 grid points wide. Can be as long as you want after that.

Platform: A platform has a width of min 2 grid points wide and can only be 1grid point height.

Space on the Y axis between floor and platform or platform and platform should be 4 grid points. For example the floor is on row 1 the platform should be on row 5. If you want a platform higher the next platform will be on row 9.

On the X axis the max spacing between the platforms will be 18 grid points. So if the end of platform one is on column 2 than the max placement of platform two starts on column 20.

Pickups: Pickups consist of 1 grid point. You should not mix and match pickups. Use 1 color for the chunk.
